Furniture


  •


    Tire Table - upcycled project made around an old car tire. I constructed the internal wooden frame and the legs. The surface was lasercut from thick acrylic.


  •


    Tire Stool - upcycled project made around an old car tire. I constructed the internal frame and base, both made from wood from a pallet.



  • Pallet Bookshelf - Another upcycled project, here I transformed a used delivery pallet into a bookshelf.

  •


    Wall Table This table is a wall-hung, flat-folding table, with an engraved and painted surface. The artwork on the face is inspired by japanese artist Hokusai.



  • Pallet Bookshelf II - the shelfening A bigger bookshelf made from a pallet, again with art inspired by The Great Wave off Kanagawa

Arduino


  •


    Mini CNC printer - This was a small arduino project I did, recycling old CD disc drives into a miniature printer, run by an Arduino Duemilanove - Design, construction, arduino programming.


  •


    Fly Robot - This is my drone project, currently ongoing. I designed and created a simple drone with a transparent acrylic body, run by an Arduino Uno and an Arduino motor shield. The goal of the project is to have the drone track its position so it can home on to a wireless charging pad when its battery is low - Electronic Engineering, CAD/CAM, design, construction, Arduino programming.



  • Claw (WIP) - This project is a servo motor, arduino controlled robotic arm. By interfacing it with a system of op-amps it is possible to make it read the impulses of human nerves, and when completed I hope to make it function as a full prosthesis.



Robotics


  •


    Hermes - My first robotics project, Hermes is a simple quadbot that uses an ultrasonic sensor to detect and avoid obstacles - Design, Construction, Arduino programming.



  • Braitenbug - I designed this as a present for my young neice and based it off the cybernetics thought experiment Braitenberg vehicles. The body is entirely acrylics and the goal of the project is to produce a toy that may spark an interest in electronics for children - Electronic Engineering, Design, Construction, Arduino Programming.



  • Hank is a robotic tortoise, made with Arduino, an ultrasonic sensor and servo motors, similar to Hermes. Hank's shell was 3D printed in PLA. Hank is named after another tortoise I know, who is not a robot.



  • Ulie - a robotics project still under development, when finished we are hoping Ulie will be an intelligent robotic companion. Ulie is built on the Raspberry Pi and uses face detection and audio processing to interact with humans.
